fNIRS Research Team

This project develops classifiers and neural networks to analyze fNIRS signals for distinguishing between healthy controls and individuals with Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI). Data preprocessing, feature extraction, and advanced classification algorithms are implemented by our team at the Laboratory of Biosignal Acquisition & Neurophysics, University of West Attica (UNIWA).
