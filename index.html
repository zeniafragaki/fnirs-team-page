About the Lab

We analyze fNIRS signals using advanced classifiers and neural networks to detect Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI). Our group specializes in preprocessing, feature extraction, and AI model development at UNIWA’s Laboratory of Biosignal Acquisition & Neurophysics.
