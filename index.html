About the Lab

We develop advanced classifiers and neural networks to analyze fNIRS signals for distinguishing between healthy individuals and those with Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI). Our research includes signal preprocessing, feature extraction, and model evaluation using state-of-the-art AI techniques. Based at the Laboratory of Biosignal Acquisition & Neurophysics, University of West Attica (UNIWA).
